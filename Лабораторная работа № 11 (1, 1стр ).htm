The Sims 3

Create game "The Sims 3"

Development of the third simulator fully began in 2006, under the leadership of Rod Humble. This is also the first game in the Sims series that was not developed by will Wright, the founder of the Sims and the Sims 2. the main task of the developers was to create an open game world, which was deprived of the Sims 2, which in turn was its most serious drawback.The simulator was mostly positively reviewed by critics. They recognized the game's advantage over the second part and mainly praised it for its open game world, improved visuals, and improved character behavior

Plot

The game is a virtual sandbox where the player can control the characters ' lives. In the basic simulator without add-ons there is a town called sunset Valley, which in the story of the game was founded by the Goth and Landgraab families, but later the Alto family settled there, which led to a conflict between the families.

Popularity

On October 31, 2008, during the US presidential election the developers released a trailer parody of the election, which featured John McCain, Barack Obama, Sarah Palin and Joe Biden.
